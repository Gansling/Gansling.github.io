Jalissa Lungrud

Born into a small mining villlage in the northern mountains of Ismarak, tragedy struck when Jalissa was just a young boy. During an accident clearing a new passageway, a large number of miners were killed, including his parents. The leader of the local monastery, a man by the name of Dervahn, dispatched help to the tragedy-stricken town, but it was too little too late. Unable to do anything else, he took the young Jalissa into his custody, raising him at the monastery. However, as he entered into adulthood, Jalissa was shaken by the realization that the monastery he had devoted his life to was in actuality a cult. After being forced to kill one of his friends during his escape, Jalissa vowed to never again take another life.
