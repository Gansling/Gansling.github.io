Soth, the Slayer of Ghost

Born to a small nomadic tribe in northern Ismarak, Soth was raised all his life to be the next shaman for the tribe. However, as he reached adulthood, his fate would take an unexpected turn. A great black dragon made it's roost at the top of the mountain, causing the sacred bear Ghost to flee towards the foothills. As she encountered a pair of the tribe's hunters, she killed them in her terrified desire to flee. Soth volunteered to slay the rampaging spirit bear, and after killing her ate her heart in a tribal ritual. Consuming her heart caused her spirit to inhabit his body, preventing him from being able to experience the spirit visions necessary for him to become the next shaman.
